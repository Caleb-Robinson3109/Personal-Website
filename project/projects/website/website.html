Introduction

Origanally I had wanted a place to put the game I had made, but after getting into it the project evolved into a full portfolio website. The idea of hosting a server on my own hardware was very appealing to me. After playing around with Apache on my laptop I was sold. I decided on the LAMP framework since it fit my needs for this website, and the use of those technologies in general.
